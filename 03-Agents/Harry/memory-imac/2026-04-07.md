## Critical Findings

### 1. Title Optimization Issues

**Current State (Estimated):**
- Titles likely under-optimized for Amazon's A9 algorithm
- Missing key search volume drivers

**Required Fixes (Priority: HIGH):**
| Issue | Current | Recommended |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| Title format | Inconsistent | Brand + Design + "for" + Device + Model + Key Feature |
| Character usage | <80 chars | 150-180 chars (max 200) |
| Device specificity | Generic | Exact model numbers (iPhone 15 Pro Max, not just "iPhone") |
| Keywords missing | — | "shockproof," "slim fit," "wireless charging compatible," "raised edges" |

**Example Optimized Title:**
```
Head Case Designs Official NFL Miami Dolphins Logo Soft Gel Case Compatible with Apple iPhone 15 Pro Max (2023) Shockproof Slim Fit Wireless Charging Compatible
```

### 2. Backend Search Terms (Hidden Keywords)

**Critical Compliance Issue:**
- Amazon explicitly prohibits brand names in backend search terms (including "iPhone," "Samsung Galaxy")
- Risk: Listing suppression or account health violations

**Recommended Backend Keywords (250 bytes max):**
```
football phone case nfl licensed official team logo sports fan merchandise protective cover durable lightweight anti scratch fingerprint resistant precise cutouts easy access
```

**What to AVOID in backend:**
- ❌ iPhone, Samsung, Galaxy, Apple, MagSafe
- ❌ Competitor brand names
- ❌ Temporary terms ("new," "sale")
- ❌ Subjective claims ("best," "top rated")

### 3. Bullet Points (5-Point Structure)

**Current gaps identified:**
- Missing feature-benefit structure
- No device compatibility clarity
- No care instructions

**Recommended Bullet Template:**

| Bullet | Content | Keywords Included |
|--------|---------|-------------------|
| 1 | **Officially Licensed** — 100% authentic NFL/WWE/NBA artwork. Show your team pride with officially approved designs. | licensed, authentic, official |
| 2 | **Precision Fit** — Engineered for [Exact Device Model]. Full access to all ports, buttons, cameras, and wireless charging. | precision fit, wireless charging, camera protection |
| 3 | **Durable Protection** — Soft gel TPU material absorbs shocks and impacts. Raised edges protect screen and camera lens. | shockproof, TPU, drop protection, raised bezel |
| 4 | **Slim & Lightweight** — Adds minimal bulk while maintaining protection. Easy grip texture prevents slips. | slim profile, lightweight, grip texture |
| 5 | **Easy Installation** — Flexible material allows easy on/off. Compatible with wireless chargers and most screen protectors. | easy install, wireless charger compatible |
