# Head Case Designs — Shipping & Returns Policy

## UK Deliveries
- **Standard (Royal Mail 2nd Class):** 2-3 working days. Allow 10 working days before contacting CS. FREE.
- **Express:** 1-2 working days. Order before 8pm for same-day dispatch. £6.95.
- No weekend/public holiday guaranteed delivery.
- Personalized items add 3-5 extra days regardless of shipping method.

## International Deliveries
- **Standard (5-7 working days):** Untracked via local mail. Allow up to 20 working days. Order must be under 2kg and under £150.
- **Express (5-7 working days):** Fully tracked. Cost varies by weight/volume.
- Untracked orders: Must notify within 40 days of shipment if not received.

## Customs & Duty
- Items ship from UK, Germany, and US.
- Duty/tax/clearance charges are the customer's responsibility.
- Cannot advise on specific country thresholds.

## Returns Policy
- **30-day return window** from receipt.
- Must be in original condition, unworn/unused, with original tags and labels.
- Items inspected upon arrival before refund processed.
- **Refund processing: up to 5 working days.** Confirmation email sent.
- **Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility.**

## Order Dispatch
- Usually within 1 business day.
- Delays possible for: payment card checks (fraud prevention) or personalized items (3-5 extra days).
