# Head Case Designs — FAQ / Common Customer Queries

## Order Status / Where Is My Order (WISMO)
- Standard UK: Allow up to 10 working days
- Express UK: 1-2 working days
- International Standard: Allow up to 20 working days
- International Express: 5-7 working days, fully tracked
- Personalized items: Add 3-5 days to dispatch time
- Orders typically dispatched within 1 business day

## Returns
- 30-day return window from date of receipt
- Items must be in original condition with tags
- Return shipping paid by customer
- Refund processed within 5 working days of receiving return
- Confirmation email sent once refund processed

## Wrong Item / Damaged Item
- Contact customer service with order number and photos
- We will arrange replacement or refund
- Do not return damaged items until instructed

## Personalization
- Personalized items take an additional 3-5 days to dispatch
- Cannot be returned unless defective
- Check spelling/details carefully before ordering

## Payment Issues
- We may perform additional payment card checks (fraud prevention)
- This can delay dispatch by 1-2 days
- Contact us if your payment was declined

## International Customs/Duties
- Customer responsible for all customs and duty charges
- Charges vary by country
- We cannot advise on specific rates
- Items ship from UK, Germany, or US depending on location

## Warranty / Quality
- All products printed on high-quality materials
- If defective on arrival, contact us for replacement

## Cancel / Modify Order
- Contact us ASAP — orders dispatch within 1 business day
- Once dispatched, cannot be modified
- Can return under 30-day policy instead
