### CRITICAL FINDING: Prime ROI is MUCH stronger than initial calculation

**Current FBM Economics (from 90-day analysis):**
- Revenue/order: $32.09 (not $27.74 as estimated)
- Reduced Shipping uptake: 65.8% (extremely high)
- iPhone 17: 81.9% Reduced uptake, 26.4% of search traffic

**Prime Breakeven (with search volume factored in):**
- Conservative: Needs only +1.65% traffic to beat FBM
- Optimistic: Needs only +0.66% traffic to beat FBM
- Amazon Prime badge typically gives +5-10% algorithmic boost = FAR above breakeven

**Search Volume Sensitivity (If iPhone 17 gets +50% traffic from Prime algorithm):**
- iPhone 17 @ 20% search volume: Prime wins by +12.4%

- iPhone 17 @ 60% search volume: Prime wins by +30.7%

**Phase 1 (Immediate):** Prime on iPhone 17
- Largest concentrated traffic pool after older iPhones
- Highest Reduced Shipping affinity already (81.9%)
- Algorithmic boost will have maximum impact
- Expected revenue lift: +15-25% on this segment

**Phase 2 (2 weeks):** A/B test Prime on iPhone 16 vs FBM
- Validate actual traffic & conversion lift

**Keep as FBM:** Older iPhones (37.4% of traffic)
