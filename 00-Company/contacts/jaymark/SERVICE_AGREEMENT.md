The Service Provider shall serve as **Technical Builder** for Ecell Global, responsible for:

- **Database Architecture** — PostgreSQL schema design, migrations, RLS policies
- **Fulfillment Systems** — Carrier integrations (Evri, Royal Mail), order management portals
- **Marketplace Tooling** — Listing pipelines, content management, API integrations
- **Testing & Deployment** — QA, staging verification, production releases

Current active projects include: Fulfillment Portal, ecell.app, Zero 2.0 replacement, OnBuy integration.

## 5. Intellectual Property

All work product — including code, designs, documentation, databases, and architectures — created during this engagement is the **exclusive property of Ecell Global Ltd**. The Service Provider retains no rights to work product created under this agreement.

## 6. Confidentiality

The Service Provider shall not disclose, share, or use for personal benefit any:
- Business strategies, financial data, or revenue figures
- API keys, credentials, tokens, or access codes
- Customer data, order information, or supplier details
- Proprietary tools, pipelines, or automation systems
- License agreements or brand partnership details

This obligation survives termination of this agreement for a period of **2 years**.

## 7. Data & Security

- No Company data shall be stored on personal devices beyond active working copies
- All credentials must be stored securely (password manager, not plaintext)
- Report any suspected security breach to the CEO within 24 hours
- Do not share access credentials with any third party

## 8. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with **30 days written notice**. The Company may terminate immediately for:
- Breach of confidentiality
- Unauthorized access or data misuse
- Failure to deliver without communication for 5+ consecutive business days
- Gross misconduct

Upon termination, the Service Provider shall: return all Company assets, revoke personal access to all systems, and deliver all work-in-progress with documentation.
