# Refined Rebrand Names: Tech Accessories Focus
## Ecell Global → Modern B2B Identity

**Criteria Update:** Names must signal "tech accessories" (phone cases, device protection) — not abstract tech/SaaS.

---

## 🏆 TOP RECOMMENDATIONS

### 1. **CELLECTA** ⭐ (Modernize Cellular)
- **Concept:** "Cellular" + "Select" (curated quality)
- **Why it works:** Modernizes E-Cellular for 2024, sophisticated, B2B appropriate
- **Domain:** Cellecta.com (likely available)
- **B2B Rating:** 8/10
- **Tagline:** *"Curated Device Protection"*

### 2. **DEVICE SHIELD** ⭐ (Protection Focus)
- **Concept:** Clear protection messaging
- **Why it works:** Immediately says what you do, corporate trust, IT-friendly
- **Domain:** DeviceShield.com (may be taken, try DeviceShield.io)
- **B2B Rating:** 9/10
- **Tagline:** *"Enterprise Device Protection"*

### 3. **ASSET GUARD** ⭐ (B2B Asset Focus)
- **Concept:** Corporate asset protection (not just "cases")
- **Why it works:** Speaks IT procurement language, elevates from "accessory" to "asset protection"
- **Domain:** AssetGuard.com (likely taken, try AssetGuard.io)
- **B2B Rating:** 9/10
- **Tagline:** *"Protecting Corporate Assets"*

### 4. **SNAP SAFE** (Snap Connection)
- **Concept:** Play on "snap-on" + safety
- **Why it works:** Satisfying action, easy to remember, consumer-friendly but works B2B
- **Domain:** SnapSafe.com (taken), SnapSafe.io (maybe)
- **B2B Rating:** 7/10
- **Tagline:** *"Snap-On Protection"*

### 5. **CORE CELL** (Cellular Core)
- **Concept:** Protects the core of cellular devices
- **Why it works:** Short, memorable, modernizes Ecell concept
- **Domain:** CoreCell.com (likely taken)
- **B2B Rating:** 9/10
- **Tagline:** *"Core Protection for Mobile Devices"*

## 🎯 STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATION

### For Core Ecell Business (Device Accessories):

**FIRST CHOICE: Cellecta**
- Modernizes "E-Cellular" without being literal
- Sophisticated for B2B but not stuffy
- Suggests curated quality (justifies $10 value positioning)
- Domain likely available
- Can expand beyond cases (Cellecta Accessories, Cellecta Enterprise)

**ALTERNATIVE: AssetGuard**
- Best if targeting IT procurement primarily
- Elevates conversation from "buying cases" to "protecting assets"
- Corporate, trustworthy
- Slightly more "security company" than "accessory company"

**ALTERNATIVE: DeviceShield**
- Most direct/descriptive
- Immediately clear what you do
- May be too literal for premium positioning

---

## 🚀 FOR THE SPIN-OFF (SaaS/Royalty Reports):

Keep the AI-focused names like:
- **Synapse** (for AI/automation software)
- **Aegis.ai** (for security/tech SaaS)
- **NeuralCase** (for design automation)

This creates a **house of brands**:
- **Cellecta** = Device accessories (physical products)
- **Synapse** = AI software/SaaS (digital products)
