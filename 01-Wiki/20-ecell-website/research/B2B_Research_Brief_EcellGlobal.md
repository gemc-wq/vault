## 🎨 STYLE GUIDE DIRECTION

### Visual Identity Goals:
- **Not:** Cheap, consumer-retail, trendy/faddy
- **Yes:** Precision, intelligence, partnership, value-engineered

### Color Palette Direction:
- Primary: Industrial blue (trust, tech) + Deep charcoal (sophistication)
- Accent: Electric/cobalt blue (AI, innovation)
- Avoid: Bright primary colors (feels cheap), pastels (feels consumer)

### Typography Direction:
- Sans-serif, geometric (modern, tech-forward)
- Clean, plenty of whitespace (Apple influence)
- Clear hierarchy (information architecture focus)

### Photography Direction:
- High-production facility shots (manufacturing credibility)
- Clean product photography (Apple quality)
- Corporate lifestyle (professional, not playful)
- Technical detail shots (materials, precision)
