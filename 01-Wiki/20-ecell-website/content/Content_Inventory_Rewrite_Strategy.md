## 🎨 VOICE & TONE GUIDELINES (Draft)

### For Ecell Tech B2B:

**DO:**
- Lead with business outcomes (ROI, efficiency)
- Use professional but accessible language
- Emphasize scale and reliability
- Include data and metrics
- Focus on partnership, not transactions

**DON'T:**
- Consumer lifestyle language
- "Enrich digital lifestyle" (too B2C)
- Overly casual or playful
- Focus on design/creativity alone
- Ignore the business buyer

**EXAMPLE TRANSFORMATIONS:**

❌ OLD (Consumer):  
"Our core purpose is to enrich people's digital lifestyle by providing and delivering products driven by creativity and innovation."

✅ NEW (B2B):  
"Ecell Tech delivers enterprise-grade device protection through AI-powered manufacturing—providing strategic value, consistent quality, and scalable partnership for businesses worldwide."
