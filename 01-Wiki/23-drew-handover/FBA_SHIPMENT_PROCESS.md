## 10-Step FBA Shipment Process

### Step 1 — Upload SKU Template
- Go to Inventory → Shipments → Send to Amazon
- Click File upload → Generate and download template
- Populate template with valid FBA SKUs
- Click Upload completed file

### Step 2 — Resolve Errors
- Fix any errors (prep information, dimensions, weights)
- This is usually the most time-consuming step
- Click Pack individual units

### Step 3 — Box Configuration
- Click "Multiple boxes will be needed" → Confirm
- Click Upload Excel file (.xls)
- Enter number of boxes
- Click Generate Excel file

### Step 4 — Upload Packing List
- Populate the generated Excel packing list
- Click Upload and validate file

### Step 5 — Confirm
- Click Confirm and continue

### Step 6 — Fulfillment Center Selection
- Click "Amazon fulfillment Center" and "Minimal Splits (East Region)"

### Step 7 — Shipping Method
- Click "Small parcel delivery (SPD)" and "UPS (Amazon partnered carrier)"

### Step 8 — Accept Charges
- Click Accept charges and confirm shipping

### Step 9 — Print Labels
- Select "Thermal printing - 4 x 6 inches"
- Click Print

### Step 10 — Track
- Click View tracking details
