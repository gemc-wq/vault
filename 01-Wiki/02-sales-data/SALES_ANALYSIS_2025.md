## Key Insights for Chatbot & Business

### 1. Geo-Aware Recommendations (Chatbot)
- **US visitors:** Lead with Harry Potter, Peanuts/Snoopy, WWE, Gilmore Girls, NCAA/NFL
- **UK visitors:** Lead with football kits (LFC, Arsenal, Chelsea, Spurs, Man City), then Harry Potter
- **EU visitors:** Lead with football (Barcelona, Inter Milan, local clubs), Harry Potter, then branded
- **JP visitors:** Mixed — no single dominant brand

### 2. Product Type Diversification
- Desk mats (HDMWH) are a major growth category — HP Marauder's Map is the #2 SKU overall
- PS5/gaming controller skins (H8939) sell extremely well
- Echo Dot accessories (H8940) — Naruto on Echo Dot is the #1 individual SKU!
- This isn't just a phone case business anymore

### 3. Seasonal Planning
- Q4 = 30% of annual revenue in 2 months
- Need inventory buildup starting September
- Black Friday / Christmas promotions should focus on top designs per region

### 4. Brand Power Rankings
1. **Football clubs** (combined) — #1 overall
2. **Harry Potter** — #1 in US, strong everywhere
3. **Peanuts/Snoopy** — Strong US, moderate UK/EU
4. **WWE** — Strong US
5. **FC Barcelona** — Strong EU
6. **Naruto** — Strong in accessories (desk mats, Echo Dot)
7. **Inter Milan** — EU niche
8. **Gilmore Girls** — US niche
