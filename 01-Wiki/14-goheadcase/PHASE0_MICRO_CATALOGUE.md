## Executive Summary

Revenue data from Amazon UK (4 months, £541K tracked) confirms:
- **Football/Sports dominates UK revenue** — £417K (77% of tracked)
- **Entertainment is the #2 vertical** — £94K (17%)
- **Anime is emerging but small** — £10K (2%) — growth bet, not revenue anchor yet
- **Football clubs convert at 3.2–4.3%** vs entertainment at 1.2–2.2% — fans buy with intent

**Recommendation:** Launch Sports microsite first (UK), Entertainment second, Anime third.
