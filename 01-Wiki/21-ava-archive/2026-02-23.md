## 1) Amazon — “Best Sellers in Cell Phone Basic Cases” (signal scan)
Source: Amazon Best Sellers page (Cell Phone Basic Cases) viewed at ~2am ET.

### What’s winning right now (pattern-level)
Even when not “licensed,” the top of Amazon is heavily concentrated around **function-led designs**:
- **MagSafe compatibility** as table-stakes (clear cases w/ MagSafe ring; “MagFit/HaloLock”-type positioning)
- **Clear / minimalist** aesthetics (show phone color; often TPU + PC)
- **Camera protection** (raised lips, camera lens guards; strong in thumbnails)
- **Kickstand / ring / grip utility** (magnetic ring stands, wallet-kickstand hybrids)
- **Value pricing** (many best sellers look like $9–$25 “utility-first” buys)

### Why this matters for GoHeadCase
Licensed art alone isn’t enough on Amazon anymore—**buyers filter by feature**. The winners combine:
> “licensed/design identity” + “MagSafe + camera protection + grip/kickstand”

**Actionable gap:** Offer **licensed-ready designs** (or licensable original art styles that match fandom aesthetics) on **MagSafe + impact** builds, and highlight camera protection + MagSafe in the first image.
