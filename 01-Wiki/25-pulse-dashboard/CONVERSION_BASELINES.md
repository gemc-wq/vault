## KEY FINDING

**Best sellers ≠ Best converters.** Zero overlap between top 10 by revenue and top 10 by conversion. Revenue is driven by SESSION VOLUME, not conversion efficiency.

**Implication:** There is significant untapped revenue in:
1. **Price increases on Stars** — market accepts current price, will likely accept $2-3 more
2. **Price decreases on Question Marks** — or fix the shipping/listing barrier
3. **PPC on Cash Cows** — converts well, just needs traffic

## Dynamic Pricing AutoResearch Loop (Proposed)

```
1. Pull conversion data weekly
2. Classify each ASIN into quadrant (Star/QMark/CashCow/Dog)
3. For Q-Marks: test price reduction ($19.95 → $17.95) for 14 days
4. For Stars: test price increase ($19.95 → $21.95) for 14 days
5. For Cash Cows: increase PPC spend, maintain price
6. Measure conversion change after 14-day attribution window
7. Keep winners, revert losers
8. Log learnings → inform next cycle
```

**Guardrails:**
- Never drop below COGS + royalty floor ($8-10 per unit)
- Maximum 20 SKUs per test cycle (statistical significance)
- Shipping cost test: $24.95 with free shipping vs $19.95 + $6.99
