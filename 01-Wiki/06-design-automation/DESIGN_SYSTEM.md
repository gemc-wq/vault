# Design Brief — “Casetify × OtterBox Business” (eCELL Global)

## Positioning target
We must sit **between**:
- **Casetify** = style/culture-first, design-led, visually premium
- **OtterBox Business** = corporate/procurement-first, rugged credibility, functional protection

**eCELL Global’s lane:** *Cost-effective corporate branding with credible protection and massive licensed/design scale.*

## What we win on
- **Price advantage** vs premium style brands
- **Branding at scale** (corporate programs, fleets, campaigns)
- **Speed + on-demand** (low inventory risk, faster refresh)
- **Design + licensed content** (when relevant)

## What we are not claiming (be honest)
- We are **not** the most rugged / most extreme-protection brand (don’t over-claim OtterBox-level engineering unless validated).
- We win on **value + design + operational capability**.

## Message hierarchy (homepage + design system)
1) **Primary:** Style meets Corporate.
2) **Secondary:** Protection with design — at global scale.
3) **Proof:** price/value, turnaround, devices supported, markets, capabilities.

## Visual direction (UI)
### Feel
- **Corporate backbone:** clean grid, strong whitespace, calm typography, clear navigation
- **Style overlay:** bold imagery blocks, vibrant category tiles, modern motion, punchy headlines

### Layout rules
- Enterprise-safe structure: short sections, clear headings, “proof modules” every ~2 scrolls.
- Use **product imagery** + **design variety** as the primary visual proof.

### Color + type guidance
- Base palette: deep neutral + white for trust
- Accent: electric blue + occasional cultural accents (used sparingly)
- Type: premium grotesk + clean UI sans (avoid playful consumer fonts)

## Copy tone
- Confident, specific, benefits-first.
- Replace hype with operational truth: *on-demand, turnaround, fleet coverage, program execution.*

## Proof modules (must-haves)
- Metrics bar (SKUs, devices, turnaround, markets)
- “How it works” flow for corporate programs
- QA/testing language (accurate + defensible)
- Partner/logo display with approvals (“selected partners” if needed)

## Component wishlist
- Category browser (Casetify-like)
- Enterprise solution cards (OtterBox Business-like)
- Configurator entry point (our differentiator)
- AI concierge entry (our “leading edge” proof)
