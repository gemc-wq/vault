## S3 Image Standard
All images must follow a standardized 6-position structure:
1. **1 Hero image** — primary product shot
2. **3 Feature images** — detail/feature callouts
3. **2 Lifestyle images** — in-context/lifestyle shots

New case uploads must conform to this 6-position set before listing.
