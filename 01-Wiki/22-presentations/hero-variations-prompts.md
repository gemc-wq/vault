# Ecell — Hero Image (HQ Render) Variations + Nano Banana Pro Prompts

Goal: recreate the “product family collage” hero in a clean, premium **Industrial Authority** style (light-mode friendly), with reserved negative space for headline + rolling brand banner.

## Global rules (apply to all)
- **Unbranded** devices/accessories; no logos, no Apple marks, no UI screens.
- Avoid neon/cyberpunk; keep palette: off-white, light gray, graphite, steel, with optional **deep navy** accent.
- Lighting: premium studio soft key + controlled rim; realistic microtexture; clean contact shadows.
- Always reserve: **top-left negative space** (headline) + **bottom band** (logo ticker).

**Recommended universal negative prompt:**
> text, watermark, logo, brand marks, neon colors, messy clutter, fingerprints, dust blobs, extreme wide angle distortion, oversaturated, harsh glare, cartoon, lowpoly, unrealistic proportions, floating products, busy background, patterned backdrop

Clean industrial authority aesthetic, subtle deep blue accent on one element, warm neutral background, soft key light from upper left, gentle rim light from right, crisp contact shadows, realistic microtexture, high-end CGI, ray-traced reflections, 50mm lens look, shallow depth of field but all products readable, ultra clean composition with large negative space in the top-left for headline and a smaller strip beneath for logo ticker. 8k, studio, premium, minimal.
