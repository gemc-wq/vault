### Current Workflow Understanding

**Process Flow:**
1. Creative sends email with raw PSD + Google Sheet tracker
2. Replicator downloads assets, creates folder structure
3. Layout designs in Smart Objects (never rasterize!)
4. Name groups: `001_MIRACULOUS_LAD`, `002_MIRACULOUS_CAT`, etc.
5. Run JSX script that loops through groups
6. Script calls Photoshop actions (NEWSKIN, SERIESX, etc.)
7. Auto-export mockups to designated folders
8. QC check and handoff

**Products Supported:**
- PS5SCS — PS5 Standard Console Skin
- PS5SDCS — PS5 Slim Digital Console Skin
- DS5EGCT — DualSense Edge Controller
- ASROGA — Asus ROG Ally
- STMDECK — Steam Deck

**Printers/Quality Tiers:**
- CAT — Catalog Quality
- HPA — High Print Accuracy
- LAD — Listing Ad
