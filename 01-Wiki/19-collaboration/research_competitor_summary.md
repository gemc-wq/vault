## Market Positioning Analysis

### OtterBox Business Strategy
- **Headline positioning:** "Built for Business"
- **Value prop:** Durability, warranty, enterprise support
- **Pricing:** Premium ($40-70 retail equivalent)
- **MOQ:** High (typically 100+ units)
- **Customization:** Logo only, limited options
- **Lead time:** 4-6 weeks

### CASETiFY B2B Strategy  
- **Headline positioning:** "Custom Cases for Teams"
- **Value prop:** Design variety, trendy aesthetics
- **Pricing:** Mid-premium ($35-60)
- **MOQ:** Moderate (25+ units)
- **Customization:** Extensive design library
- **Lead time:** 2-3 weeks

### iPromo Strategy
- **Headline positioning:** "Custom Promotional Products"
- **Value prop:** One-stop promotional shop
- **Pricing:** Low-mid ($5-20)
- **MOQ:** Low (12+ units typical)
- **Customization:** Screen printing, basic options
- **Lead time:** 2-4 weeks
- **Weakness:** Not device-specific, quality issues

## Messaging Gaps Identified

### What Competitors DON'T Say:

1. **Equipment Transparency** — None showcase manufacturing equipment (Mimaki, Canon)
2. **AI/Automation** — Zero competitors mention AI in production workflow
3. **True Zero MOQ** — Everyone has minimums; Ecell's "order 1" is unique
4. **IT Asset Management** — No one addresses fleet deployment challenges
5. **Integration APIs** — Limited/no technical integration options
6. **Sustainability Data** — Vague claims, no concrete metrics
