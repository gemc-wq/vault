## Four-Test Framework
All operational actions must pass:
1. **North Star test**: Does it increase Coverage, Speed, or Intelligence?
2. **Data test**: Is the decision supported by data?
3. **Risk test**: What are the potential downsides?
4. **Effort/ROI test**: Is the expected return worth the required effort?

## Review Process
- All major architecture and strategy documents must be run through the Advisor tool (Opus) for review before finalization.
