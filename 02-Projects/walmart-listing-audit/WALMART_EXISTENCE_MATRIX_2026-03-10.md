- Designs that DO exist have <20% device coverage
- Combined: Walmart has maybe **3-5% of the addressable catalog** that's on Amazon

### 2. Revenue Opportunity
- Conservative estimate: If Walmart converts at even 5% of Amazon's rate for the same designs, expanding the top 100 missing designs could add $20-40K/quarter

### 3. Two-Pronged Fix Needed
1. **Catalog expansion:** List the top 100+ revenue designs currently missing
2. **Device expansion:** For 726 existing designs, expand to match Amazon device coverage
3. **Content optimization:** Fix titles (99.9% over-length) to improve search ranking
