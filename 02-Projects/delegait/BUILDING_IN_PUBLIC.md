## The Competitive Moat

1. **We eat our own cooking.** Every feature we sell, we run ourselves. Show, don't tell.
2. **NemoClaw = on-prem option.** Client data never leaves their infrastructure. No agency can offer that.
3. **Ecell Global = proof at scale.** "We manage $5M in marketplace revenue with this system." Not a startup pitch — a track record.
4. **Cost structure is untouchable.** Zero employees = infinite margins. Competitors with teams of 10 can't match our pricing.
