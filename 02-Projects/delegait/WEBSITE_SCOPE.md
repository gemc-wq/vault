- **Feel:** "This company is run by AI and it looks better than most agencies."

### PAGE 6: CONTACT (/contact)

#### AI Chatbot (primary)
The contact page IS the product demo. The chatbot qualifies visitors in real-time:

Flow:
1. "Hey! Tell me about your business in one sentence."
2. "How many people on your team?"
3. "What's eating most of your time right now?" (multi-select: admin, sales, follow-ups, content, invoicing)
4. "What tools are you currently using?" (multi-select: spreadsheets, HubSpot, QuickBooks, nothing)
5. Score → If qualified: "Great — let me find a time for a quick call with Cem." → Calendly embed
6. If not qualified: "Here's our free AI Readiness Assessment" → email capture
