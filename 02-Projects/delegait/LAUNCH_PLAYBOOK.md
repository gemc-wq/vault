## The Philosophy

"If we can't build a profitable business with AI alone, we have no business selling AI to others."

Every tool, every workflow, every system we sell — NemoClaw runs it for Delegait first. Client asks "does this actually work?" You show them your own dashboard.

## Week 1: Foundation (NemoClaw builds Delegait)

### Day 1-2: Identity
- delegait.com landing page (v0/Lovable, deployed on Vercel)
- Brand kit: logo, colors, fonts (AI-generated, human-approved)
- @delegait social profiles claimed and branded
- Business email: cem@delegait.com (or hello@)

### Day 3-4: Storefront
- AI chatbot on the site ("Tell us about your business" → qualification quiz)
- Calendly/Cal.com booking linked to Cem's calendar
- CRM (HubSpot free or Pipedrive) — pipeline stages configured
- Stripe connected for payments

### Day 5-7: Content Engine
- 3 launch blog posts targeting "AI for small business [city]" keywords
- First week of social content (LinkedIn + X) — scheduled
- "AI Readiness Assessment" — a free quiz that qualifies leads AND generates content

**Cost:** $0 in labor. ~$200 in tools (domain + hosting + Stripe).

## Week 2: Outreach (NemoClaw finds customers)

### ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- Professional services firm (law, accounting, marketing agency, consulting)
- 2-20 employees
- Orlando metro + remote
- Revenue $500K-$5M
- Currently using: spreadsheets, Gmail, maybe QuickBooks
- Feeling: overwhelmed, curious about AI, scared to start

### Outreach Machine

| Channel | What NemoClaw Does | Target |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | Profile visits → connect → value message sequence (3-touch) | 50 prospects/week |
| Cold email | AI-personalized 5-touch sequences referencing their specific business | 100 prospects/week |
| X/Twitter | Engage with #smallbusiness #AIautomation conversations, share insights | Daily |
| Local | Scrape Orlando Chamber of Commerce, BNI groups, local business directories | 200 targets |
| Content | "How I launched a business with zero employees" thread on LinkedIn | Viral play |

### The pitch IS the proof:
"I built this entire outreach system using AI. No employees, no agency. I'm reaching you right now because of it. Want one for your business?"
