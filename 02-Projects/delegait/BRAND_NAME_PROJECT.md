# DelegAIte — Brand Rename Project
*Created: April 2026 | Owner: Cem Celikkol*

---

## The Brief

Rename DelegAIte to a brand that reflects:
- **Agentic AI** — agents that act, plan, execute autonomously
- **Edge + disruption** — forward-thinking, not classic or consultancy
- **Small business resonance** — feels like a cheat code, a boost, a GSD machine
- **Short** — one word preferred, two words maximum
- **Ownable** — low IP conflict risk, available as .ai domain

---

## The Brand Truth

> *"This will get shit done and make me money."*

That is the message a small business owner should feel the moment they hear the name. Not "this is clever AI." Not "this is a platform." GSD. Revenue. Results.

---

## Target Customer

**US & UK small businesses:**
- E-commerce founders (Amazon, Shopify, Etsy) — 1-20 person teams
- Professional services (law, accounting, agencies, consultants) — 2-20 people
- Revenue: $500K–$5M
- Pain: drowning in admin, can't afford a team, curious about AI but don't know where to start
- Desire: compete with bigger companies without hiring

---

## Cem's Positioning (The Proof Behind the Brand)

- 2005 — Founded Ecell, pre-iPhone D2C mobile accessories retailer (UK)
- 2007 — Relocated to Shenzhen, China. Built procurement + logistics on the ground. Wire transfers, no buyer protection. Built supplier trust the hard way.
- 2011 — First POD manufacturing site in Shenzhen. Founded Head Case Designs.
- 2013 — Expanded to Philippines. Second manufacturing + fulfilment hub.
- 2024 — Replaced manual business functions with AI agents (Ava, Harry, Athena, Hermes)
- 2026 — $5M+ revenue, 7 marketplaces, AI-operated. Building DelegAIte.

**Positioning line:** *"The Autonomous Operator — built a $5M AI-run business. Now doing it for yours."*

## Recommended Direction

**Primary recommendation: GSD.ai**

Three letters. No explanation needed. Every business owner in the US and UK knows GSD. It's the message AND the brand in one. The name does all the marketing.

- Tagline: *"AI that gets shit done."*
- SMB reaction: *"That's exactly what I need."*
- Cem's story: *"I built a $5M business that GSD while I sleep. Now yours can too."*
- Domain: gsd.ai (check availability — may need getgsd.ai or gsd.co)

**Secondary recommendation: Graft**

For a more premium, boutique direction that's authentically Cem.

- British origin: "graft" = hard work, grind, doing the job
- Completely ownable in AI space
- Tagline: *"We do the graft. You take the profit."*
- Domain: graft.ai (check availability)
