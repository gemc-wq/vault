## Module 3: 🎯 SMART CRM
*"Every lead scored, tagged, and tracked — automatically"*

| What | How |
|---|---|
| AI lead qualification | Chatbot scores: budget, timeline, decision-maker, fit → Hot/Warm/Cold |
| Auto-tagging | Lead enters CRM pre-tagged by source, score, and intent |

*"Qualified leads book themselves"*

|---|---|
