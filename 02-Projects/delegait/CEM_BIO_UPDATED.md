## 1. PERSONAL + PROFESSIONAL BIO (Full Version)

Cem Celikkol is a British entrepreneur, systems thinker, and the CEO & Co-Founder of Ecell Global — a globally distributed, AI-operated licensed tech accessories company generating $5M+ in annual revenue across Amazon, Walmart, Shopify, and seven international marketplaces. He is also the founder of **DelegAIte**, an AI operations platform for small businesses in the US and UK.

His story starts earlier than most.

In **2005**, Cem incorporated Ecell — one of the UK's early direct-to-consumer online retailers of mobile phone, iPod, and mobile gaming accessories. This was the pre-iPhone era: clear crystal cases, chargers, and accessories for devices most people have long forgotten. E-commerce was new. Consumer trust was thin. Cem figured it out anyway.

In **2007**, he made a decision that would define the next two decades: he relocated to **Shenzhen, China**, and set up Ecell's procurement and logistics operation on the ground. This was not a comfortable move. Suppliers demanded wire transfers upfront — no buyer protection, no recourse, no safety net. Building supplier relationships in that environment required something money couldn't buy: presence, trust, and persistence. Cem built all three.

By **2011**, he had gone a step further — establishing Ecell's first **print-on-demand manufacturing site in Shenzhen**, and simultaneously founding **Head Case Designs**, the licensed accessories brand that would become the creative engine of the business. This was POD manufacturing before it had a name, built from scratch in a Shenzhen factory.

In **2013**, operations expanded to the **Philippines**, adding a second manufacturing and fulfilment hub — giving the business geographic resilience and round-the-clock production capacity across two continents.

Born and raised in the UK, Cem's career took him across six countries — **China, Hong Kong, Philippines, Japan, Germany** — before he relocated to **Orlando, Florida**, where he has been based for the past six years. Each stop added a layer: supply chain discipline in China, creative operations in the Philippines, precision in Japan, process engineering in Germany.

A background in **Chemical Engineering** gave him the language of process control — the idea that a well-designed system, once running, needs only monitoring, not constant intervention. That idea became his operating philosophy.

Then came AI.

Starting in 2024, Cem began systematically replacing manual business functions with intelligent agents. Today, Ecell Global is run by a team of named AI agents — **Ava** (Chief Process & Strategy Officer), **Harry** (Finance), **Athena** (Orchestrator), and **Hermes** (Sales Analytics) — operating on a nightly cron cycle, writing memory logs, compiling reports, and executing the full 10-stage pipeline from license signing to fulfillment to royalty accounting. The business runs while Cem sleeps.

The natural next step was **DelegAIte** — a spin-off born from the question: *What if every small business could have this?*

DelegAIte packages the same AI infrastructure that runs a multi-million dollar operation into bespoke automation stacks for small e-commerce brands and professional services firms in the US and UK. Not software. Not a tool. An AI workforce that operates your business.

Cem is currently based in **Orlando, FL**, building DelegAIte in public.
