## Two verticals, 80% shared infrastructure

---

## Vertical 1: SaaS Companies (5-50 employees)

### Their Pain
- Churn they don't see coming
- Support tickets eating engineering time
- Onboarding that's manual and inconsistent
- Metrics scattered across Stripe, Intercom, HubSpot, Mixpanel
- Founder still doing half the sales

### Delegait Skills

| Skill | What it does |
|---|---|
| Churn Radar | Monitors usage drop-offs, payment failures, support sentiment → alerts before they cancel |
| Support Triage | Classifies tickets, drafts responses, escalates only what needs a human |
| Onboarding Autopilot | Welcome sequences, setup check-ins, activation nudges based on product usage |
| Revenue Pulse | Weekly: MRR, churn rate, expansion revenue, LTV/CAC — one dashboard, one email |
| Pipeline Copilot | Lead scoring, follow-up sequences, meeting prep from CRM data |
| Content Engine | Blog drafts, changelog posts, social updates from product releases |

---

## Vertical 2: Professional Services (law, accounting, consulting, agencies — 2-30 people)

### Their Pain
- Billable time lost to admin
- Proposals take days to write
- Client follow-up falls through cracks
- No visibility into pipeline or utilization
- Knowledge trapped in partners' heads

### Delegait Skills

| Skill | What it does |
|---|---|
| Proposal Builder | Scope → draft proposal in minutes, branded, with pricing |
| Client Pulse | Tracks engagement, flags at-risk accounts, reminds on deliverables |
| Time Saver | Auto-categorizes calendar + email into billable/non-billable time |
| Pipeline View | Deals in progress, probability-weighted revenue forecast, weekly digest |
| Knowledge Base | Indexes past proposals, SOWs, deliverables — searchable by anyone on the team |
| Inbox Manager | Triage, prioritize, draft responses, flag deadlines buried in emails |
