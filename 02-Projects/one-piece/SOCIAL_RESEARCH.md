## What Fans Are Buying & Talking About

### Top Design Categories (by demand signals)
1. **Gear 5 Luffy** — single biggest character moment in recent One Piece history. Iridescent/glow treatment is winning (Loungefly NYCC 2025 Gear 5 collection sold out at 600–900 unit runs at $9.50–$90).
2. **Wanted Poster designs** — Luffy's wanted poster format is perennially popular. Clean, recognizable, instantly "One Piece."
3. **Dark aesthetic / black backgrounds** — Older fanbase (18–30) prefers moody, premium feel over bright colors.
4. **Chibi / cute style** — Younger/female audience. Chopper is consistently top seller in cute-style.
5. **Straw Hat crew collages** — "All 9 members" format sells to completionists.
6. **Zoro swords** — Three-sword imagery is distinctively Zoro, highly merchandisable.

### What's Resonating on Social (2024–2025)
- Gear 5 content dominates anime social media — white hair, Sun God Nika form, that iconic laugh
- Iridescent/holographic finishes + glow-in-the-dark on G5 Luffy merch = viral pickup
- Wanted poster as format (yellow-cream background, distressed border, red WANTED stamp) = instantly shareable
- Scene recreations: Shanks × Luffy emotional moments, Ace's death, Law's tragic backstory — emotional resonance converts
- "The anime fans HATE inaccurate merch" signal from Funko Pop Gear 5 backlash — fans want faithful character art, not cutified versions of serious moments

### Platform Trends
- **TikTok**: Unboxing + "rate my anime merch" content drives discovery. Speed reveals work.
- **Instagram Reels**: Character aesthetic moodboards, "my setup" lifestyle content
- **Twitter/X**: "Fits with the case" posts, case + outfit coordination, show-off culture
- **Pinterest**: Flat lay product shots, pastel/aesthetic setups

### Competitor Landscape (Non-Casetify)
- **onepiece.store**: $20–$25 price point, budget market
- **Redbubble/Etsy**: Fan-made designs, cheaper but not official — our licensed edge
- **Uniqlo + Lacoste**: Licensed apparel collabs (not cases) — shows IP is mainstream fashion-ready
- Our position: OFFICIAL license, quality head case print quality, competitive price vs Casetify ($15–$35 vs $70–$110)

## Key Insight for Creative Direction
Casetify proved the formula:
1. Black backgrounds make characters pop — stop using white
2. Gold accent = premium signal, especially for milestone/anniversary
3. Gear 5 is the #1 character moment right now — lead with it
4. Each character needs ONE definitive design (Zoro = three swords crossed, Chopper = cute form, Ace = fire)
5. Wanted poster format is evergreen and highly shareable
