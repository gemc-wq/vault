## 2. Casetify Strengths & Weaknesses

### Strengths
- **Premium brand equity**: Casetify = aspirational tech accessory brand. Hollywood celebrity associations.
- **Collector positioning**: Limited drops create FOMO, drive repeat purchases
- **Design quality**: High-resolution licensed art, gold foil options, multiple case types per design
- **Retail presence**: Studio stores in major cities create IRL touchpoints
- **Co-Lab app**: Dedicated collector platform, early access, exclusives
- **Social proof**: Millions of Instagram followers, influencer seeding, celebrity gifting
- **4 collections deep**: Built an established One Piece fanbase over 3+ years

### Weaknesses
- **Price**: $38–$112 excludes mass-market fans (18-24 demos, students, casual fans)
- **Limited SKU breadth**: Each collection focuses on ~10-15 designs, ~30-40 total SKUs
- **iOS-centric**: Heavy Apple ecosystem focus. Limited Samsung/Android coverage historically
- **No Amazon**: Zero marketplace presence — no organic discovery for non-Casetify-aware shoppers
- **Drop model friction**: Sold-out designs aren't restocked (frustrates buyers)
- **Slow characters roster**: Characters added selectively — gaps in crew coverage (Brook, Franky, Usopp underrepresented)
- **No desk mats**: Absent from gaming peripherals category
- **Price/value for casual fans**: A $45 phone case is hard to justify for someone who liked the Netflix live action
