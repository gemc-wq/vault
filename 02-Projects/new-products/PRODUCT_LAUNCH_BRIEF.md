# PRODUCT 1: MagSafe Water Bottle
## "Your Team. Your Bottle. Your Tripod."

---

### What Is It?

A premium 304 stainless steel insulated water bottle with an integrated MagSafe-compatible magnetic phone mount built into the lid. The phone mount doubles as an adjustable stand (15°–90°), turning the bottle into a portable tripod for recording, video calls, recipe following, and content creation.

Available in two print finishes:
- **UV DTF** — Full-colour licensed artwork wraps (vibrant, detailed, photo-quality)
- **Laser Engraving** — Premium etched designs on metal surface (subtle, luxury feel)

### Why Now?

- MagSafe accessory market: **$2.1B** (2024), growing **16.7% CAGR** to $8.4B by 2033
- MagSafe water bottles are a **viral TikTok category** — gym/fitness content creators are the primary driver
- "MagSafe compatible" search volume up **340% YoY**
- **Zero competitors have licensed designs** — every seller offers generic solid colours
- We have blanks in warehouse, printers in-house, licenses approved — **zero lead time**
