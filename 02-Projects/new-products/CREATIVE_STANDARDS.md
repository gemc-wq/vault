## PRODUCT 1: MagSafe Water Bottle

### Substrate Specs
- **Shape:** Cylindrical stainless steel (304 grade)
- **Sizes:** 24oz and 32oz
- **Colors available:** White, Blue, Silver, Red
- **Print area:** Wrap around bottle body (NOT the lid/MagSafe mount)

### Print Methods

#### A) UV DTF (Full Color)
- **Use for:** Colorful, detailed artwork (Harry Potter scenes, Looney Tunes characters, full-color crests)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI minimum, 600 DPI preferred
- **Color profile:** CMYK (Coated FOGRA39 or US Web Coated SWOP v2)
- **File format:** PDF (vector preferred) or TIFF (raster). NO JPG.
- **Bleed:** 3mm on all edges of the print wrap

#### B) Laser Engraving (Premium Line)
- **Use for:** Minimalist, premium designs — crests, logos, monograms, badges
- **File format:** Vector only — AI or SVG. NO raster.
- **Color:** Single color (engraved = etched into metal surface)
- **Line weight:** Minimum 0.3mm for fine detail
- **Fill areas:** Solid fills OK. Avoid gradients — they don't engrave well.
- **Best results on:** Silver/steel bottles (natural metal shows through)

### Template Dimensions

#### 24oz Bottle
| Measurement | Value |
|------------|-------|
| Bottle diameter | ~73mm |
| Circumference (print wrap) | ~229mm |
| Printable height | ~180mm |

| Bleed | 3mm outside all edges |
| **Full template with bleed** | **235mm × 186mm** |

#### 32oz Bottle

|------------|-------|

### Design Rules — Bottles

1. **Seam alignment:** The wrap has a seam (back of bottle). Place primary design element on FRONT. Logo/badge should be centered on the front face (~50% of circumference width).
2. **Avoid full 360° wraps for v1** — start with front-panel designs (~120° of the circumference = ~76mm for 24oz, ~84mm for 32oz). Simpler to align, less waste on errors.
3. **White bottle = richest colors.** Use white stock for full-color UV DTF prints.
4. **Silver bottle = laser engraving only.** Don't UV print on silver — colors look washed out on metallic.
5. **Blue/Red bottles = limited print.** White or light artwork only, OR laser engrave. Test first.
6. **Keep text above 8pt** — curved surface makes small text hard to read.
7. **No artwork within 10mm of the top or bottom edge** — these areas are hidden by lid and base.

## PRODUCT 2: Metal Wall Art (Aluminium Panel)

### Substrate Specs
- **Material:** 1mm aluminium sheet, gloss white coated (sublimation-ready)
- **Size:** 300mm × 400mm (portrait orientation standard)
- **Corners:** R2 rounded
- **Print method:** UV Flatbed
- **Mounting:** Magnetic system (80×80mm magnet on panel back + steel plate on wall)

### Template Dimensions

| Measurement | Value |
|------------|-------|
| Panel size | **300mm × 400mm** |
| Bleed | 3mm (print extends to 306mm × 406mm, trimmed) |
| Safe zone | 8mm inset from panel edge (no critical text/logos) |
| **Live area** | **284mm × 384mm** |
| Resolution | 300 DPI minimum |
| Color profile | CMYK |
| File format | PDF (vector+raster) or TIFF |

### Design Rules — Wall Art

1. **FULL BLEED designs look best.** Edge-to-edge artwork with no white border = premium look (like Displate). Design should extend to the 306×406mm bleed area.
2. **Alternatively: Bordered designs** — artwork with 10-15mm white/black border = clean gallery look. Both styles are valid.
3. **Orientation:** Default is PORTRAIT (300w × 400h). Landscape (400w × 300h) is allowed but label the file clearly.
4. **Background matters:** The aluminium base is gloss white. Unprinted areas will show as bright gloss white. Design accordingly — either fill the entire panel OR intentionally use the white as part of the design.
5. **Metallic effect:** You can leave small areas unprinted to let the aluminium show through for a metallic/silver accent. This is a unique advantage over paper/canvas prints. USE IT.
6. **Text minimum:** 10pt for body text, 18pt for headlines. Metal surface is glossy — fine text can be hard to read at viewing distance (1-2m).
7. **Magnet placement:** The 80×80mm magnet adheres to the BACK center of the panel. No design consideration needed (back is unprinted).
8. **Edge quality:** UV flatbed prints right to the edge. Ensure no critical design elements are within 3mm of the trim line.

## FILE NAMING CONVENTION

```
{PRODUCT}_{SIZE}_{LICENSE}_{DESIGN}_{METHOD}.{ext}

Examples:
WBTL_24OZ_MANCITY_CREST_UVDTF.pdf
WBTL_32OZ_BATMAN_BATLOGO_LASER.svg
MWART_3040_HPOTTER_HOGWARTS_UVFLAT.pdf
MWART_3040_SPURS_CREST_UVFLAT.pdf
```

### Product codes:
- `WBTL` = Water Bottle
- `MWART` = Metal Wall Art

### Size codes:
- `24OZ` / `32OZ` = bottle size
- `3040` = 300×400mm wall art

### License codes:
- `MANCITY` = Manchester City
- `SPURS` = Tottenham Hotspur
- `BATMAN` / `HPOTTER` / `LTUNES` = Warner Brothers sub-brands

### Method codes:
- `UVDTF` = UV DTF (full color, bottles)
- `LASER` = Laser engraving (bottles)
- `UVFLAT` = UV Flatbed (wall art)

## QC CHECKLIST (before shipping any unit)

### Water Bottles
- [ ] Print aligned (no visible seam overlap on front)
- [ ] No bubbling/peeling on UV DTF (scratch test with fingernail)
- [ ] Laser engraving depth consistent (no light/dark patches)
- [ ] MagSafe magnet holds iPhone 15 Pro Max with case (test)
- [ ] Lid seals properly (water test — invert for 30 seconds)
- [ ] Stand mechanism clicks and holds at all angles (15°-90°)
- [ ] No sharp edges on print boundary

### Wall Art
- [ ] Print covers full panel to edges (no white gaps on bleed designs)
- [ ] Colors match approved proof (compare to screen proof under daylight)
- [ ] No scratches on gloss surface
- [ ] Corners rounded (no sharp metal edges)
- [ ] Magnet adheres to back panel (centered)
- [ ] Steel plate + sticker included in packaging
- [ ] Panel holds to wall mount (weight test — panel should not slide)

## PACKAGING (v1 — minimal viable)

### Water Bottles
- Kraft box or white box, bottle-sized
- Tissue wrap or foam sleeve
- Insert card: "Licensed [Brand] MagSafe Water Bottle by Head Case Designs"
- Include MagSafe booster ring (for non-MagSafe phones) if available

### Wall Art
- Rigid cardboard mailer (DO NOT flex or fold)
- Corner protectors (foam or cardboard)
- Bubble wrap inner layer
- Insert: magnetic mount kit (magnet + steel plate + adhesive sticker)
- Instruction card: "How to mount your metal poster" (2-step: stick plate to wall → attach panel magnetically)
