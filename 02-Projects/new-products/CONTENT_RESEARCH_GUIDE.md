## PART 1: WATER BOTTLE — CONTENT THAT SELLS

### What Top Competitors Are Doing (Image Breakdown)

**magcheer (BSR #7,795 — Amazon king of MagSafe bottles):**
1. Hero: White background, bottle at 3/4 angle with phone attached showing timer app
2. Feature callouts: Infographic with arrows → "24H Cold / 12H Hot", "MagSafe Compatible", "BPA Free"
3. Lifestyle: Person at gym, bottle on floor, phone recording their deadlift
4. Scale: Bottle next to iPhone for size reference
5. Multi-angle: Front, back, top-down of lid mechanism
6. What's in the box: Bottle + booster ring + straw + cleaning brush laid out

**RhinoShield AquaStand (best product photos in category):**
1. Hero: Clean product shot, stand deployed at ~60° angle with iPhone attached
2. 4-panel lifestyle grid: Gym, kitchen (recipe following), desk (video call), outdoor (tripod selfie)
3. Stand mechanism close-up: 15°-90° angle range with measurement overlay
4. Color options: All colorways shown in single image
5. Cross-sell: Bottle + matching RhinoShield case shown together
6. Sustainability messaging: Tree planting, recycled materials callouts
7. Video: 15-second loop of phone snapping onto bottle and angle adjusting

**Ringo PRO ($55, 41 reviews but climbing fast):**
1. Hero: Matte black bottle, ultra-clean product shot
2. "Record your lifts" lifestyle: Gym floor POV, phone recording at low angle
3. "No more tripods" messaging — comparison graphic: tripod (❌) vs Ringo (✅)
4. Straw mechanism close-up
5. Content creator angle: Person filming cooking/dance with bottle as tripod

### What Ecell Must Do DIFFERENTLY (Our Licensed Edge)

**Image 1 — Hero (White background, Amazon compliant):**
- Bottle front-facing, phone attached showing the MATCHING PHONE CASE design
- The case on the phone should match the bottle design (Man City on both)
- This is the "stop the scroll" moment — nobody else shows matching case + bottle

**Image 2 — The "Fan Shot":**
- Lifestyle: Fan wearing Man City jersey, bottle on desk, watching match on phone mounted to bottle
- OR: Kid's bedroom with Harry Potter bottle on desk, Hogwarts on the screen
- The emotional hook: "This is YOUR bottle. Your team. Your fandom."

**Image 3 — Feature Infographic:**
- Callouts: MagSafe, 24H Cold/12H Hot, 304 Stainless Steel, BPA Free
- Show the stand angle range (15°-90°)
- Include: "Works with iPhone 12+" and "Booster ring included for all phones"

**Image 4 — Gym/Active Lifestyle:**
- Bottle on gym floor, phone recording a workout
- Person's feet visible in frame (POV shot)
- Caption overlay: "Record your sets. Stay hydrated. Rep your team."

**Image 5 — The Bundle Cross-Sell:**
- Bottle + matching phone case + matching wall art (if available in same design)
- "Complete the Collection" text overlay
- This is a MASSIVE differentiator — nobody else has a matching ecosystem

**Image 6 — Multi-Color/Design Options:**
- Grid showing all available designs for that license
- E.g., Man City: Crest on White, Crest on Blue, Crest Laser on Silver

**Image 7 — What's In The Box:**
- Flat lay: Bottle, lid, straw, booster ring, instruction card
- Clean, well-lit, shows customer exactly what they're getting

### Amazon Video (30 seconds — CRITICAL for conversion)
Best performing bottle videos follow this formula:
1. **0-3s:** Phone snaps onto bottle (satisfying magnetic click sound)
2. **3-8s:** Quick montage — gym, desk, kitchen, car cup holder
3. **8-15s:** Stand angle adjustment demo (portrait → landscape)
4. **15-22s:** Show the licensed design in detail (zoom on artwork)
5. **22-28s:** Cross-sell flash — matching case on phone
6. **28-30s:** Logo + "Available in [X] designs"

## PART 2: METAL WALL ART — CONTENT THAT SELLS

### What Displate Does (the $277M benchmark)

**Website Product Pages:**
- Hero: The artwork itself, full bleed, high-res
- Room mockup: Art shown on a wall in a styled room (bedroom, gaming setup, living room)
- Close-up: Metal texture visible, showing gloss/matte finish and print quality
- Size comparison: Art shown with person standing next to it (or furniture for scale)
- Mounting demo: "Stick magnet to wall → hang poster → done in 10 seconds"
- Collection view: 3-4 related designs shown as a gallery wall grouping

**What Makes Displate Content Go Viral:**
- **Gaming setup shots** — desk with RGB lighting, metal poster above monitor
- **"Room transformation" before/after** — bare wall → styled wall with metal art
- **Collection flex** — fans showing off their 5-10 piece gallery walls
- **Unboxing** — the satisfying moment of peeling off protective film
- **Magnetic snap** — the satisfying click when poster attaches to wall mount

### Trending Room Aesthetics for Wall Art (2025-2026)

| Aesthetic | Target Audience | Content Style |
|-----------|----------------|---------------|
| **Gaming setup** | Male 18-34 | Dark room, RGB lighting, art above dual monitors |
| **Dorm room** | Students 18-22 | Budget-friendly, easy mount, personality expression |
| **Man cave / sports room** | Male 25-45 | Team colors, memorabilia vibe, leather furniture |
| **Kids room** | Parents buying for kids 8-16 | Bright colors, character designs, safe mounting |
| **Minimalist** | Male/Female 25-40 | Clean lines, single statement piece, white walls |
| **Gallery wall** | Female 25-40 | Curated collection, multiple pieces, styling |

### What Ecell Must Do (Our Licensed Edge)

**Image 1 — The Art Itself (Product Shot):**
- Full panel, slight 3D perspective showing the metal thickness and rounded corners
- Clean white or dark background
- Gloss reflection visible to communicate "this is METAL, not paper"

**Image 2 — Gaming Setup Lifestyle:**
- Dark room, RGB backlit desk, dual monitors
- Man City or Batman metal art centered above monitors
- This is the #1 viral aesthetic for wall art on Reddit/Pinterest/TikTok

**Image 3 — Living Room / Bedroom Lifestyle:**
- Bright, modern room, art on white wall
- Styled with minimal furniture (plants, books, lamp)
- Shows the art "floating" off the wall (magnetic mount gap visible)

**Image 4 — The Magnetic Mount Demo:**
- 3-step infographic: 1) Stick steel plate to wall 2) Peel sticker backing 3) Snap art magnetically
- "No nails. No drilling. No damage." text overlay
- This is Displate's #1 selling point and we need to match it

**Image 5 — Gallery Wall Grouping:**
- 3-4 designs from same license shown together on a wall
- E.g., 4 Harry Potter house crests arranged in a 2×2 grid
- Shows the "collectible" angle — encourages multi-purchase

**Image 6 — Close-Up Detail:**
- Macro shot showing print quality on metal surface
- Gloss reflection, color vibrancy, sharp edges
- Communicates premium quality vs cheap paper posters

**Image 7 — Size Reference:**
- Art held by person, or shown next to common object (laptop, book)
- 300×400mm = roughly size of a laptop screen + border
- Helps buyers understand what they're getting

### Amazon Video (30 seconds)
1. **0-3s:** Package arrives, hands open box
2. **3-8s:** Reveal art panel, show metallic surface catch the light
3. **8-15s:** Mount demo — stick plate to wall, snap art on magnetically (satisfying click)
4. **15-22s:** Pull back to show art in styled room
5. **22-28s:** Gallery wall with 3-4 pieces
6. **28-30s:** Logo + "Licensed [Brand] Metal Wall Art by Head Case Designs"

## APPENDIX: IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS

### Amazon Requirements
- Minimum: 1600×1600px (for zoom)
- Format: JPEG or PNG
- Main image: Pure white background (RGB 255,255,255), product fills 85% of frame
- No text, logos, or watermarks on main image
- Additional images: Text/graphics allowed

### Social Media
- TikTok/Reels: 1080×1920px (9:16 vertical)
- Instagram Feed: 1080×1080px (1:1 square) or 1080×1350px (4:5)
- Pinterest: 1000×1500px (2:3 vertical)

### Website (GoHeadCase)
- Product page hero: 2000×2000px minimum
- Lifestyle: 1920×1080px (16:9)
