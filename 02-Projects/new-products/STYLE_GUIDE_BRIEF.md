## VISUAL IDENTITY STANDARDS

### Color Palette

**Primary (Brand):**
| Color | Hex | Use |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Head Case Black | #1A1A1A | Text, frames, backgrounds |
| Head Case White | #FFFFFF | Backgrounds, text on dark |
| Accent Gold | #C9A84C | Premium tier callouts, laser line |

**Per License (use as accent colors):**
| License | Primary | Secondary | Background Option |
|---------|---------|-----------|-------------------|
| Man City | #6CABDD (Sky Blue) | #1C2C5B (Navy) | Gradient sky blue → navy |
| Tottenham | #132257 (Navy) | #FFFFFF (White) | Navy with white accents |
| Batman | #000000 (Black) | #FDE311 (Bat Yellow) | Dark/noir with yellow pop |
| Harry Potter | #740001 (Gryffindor Red) | #D3A625 (Gold) | Parchment/stone textures |
| Looney Tunes | #FF0000 (Red) | #FFD700 (Gold) | Bright, playful, white base |

### Typography

**Headlines:** Inter Bold or Montserrat Bold (clean, modern, legible at small sizes)
**Body:** Inter Regular or Open Sans Regular
**Accent/Callouts:** Montserrat SemiBold Italic

**Do NOT use:**
- Script/handwriting fonts
- Serif fonts (too traditional for this product category)
- License-specific fonts without approval (e.g., Harry Potter font needs WB sign-off)

### Photography Style

**Lighting:**
- **Product shots:** Soft, even studio lighting. No harsh shadows. Slight gradient on background OK.
- **Lifestyle shots:** Natural light preferred. Warm tone for home settings, cooler for gym/tech.
- **Wall art room shots:** Slightly moody/cinematic. RGB ambient lighting for gaming setups.

**Angles:**
- **Bottles:** 3/4 front view (hero), straight front (alt), top-down (lid), side (stand mechanism)
- **Wall art:** Slight perspective (shows depth/thickness), straight on (shows art), side (shows float gap)

**Post-Processing:**
- Clean and natural. Don't over-saturate.
- Consistent white balance across all images in a set
- Subtle shadow under products on white backgrounds
- For lifestyle: light color grading to match the mood (warm for home, cool for gym)

## DO's AND DON'Ts

### DO ✅
- Show the licensed design PROMINENTLY — it's the reason people buy
- Use matching phone cases on phones in bottle shots — unique cross-sell nobody else has
- Show the magnetic mount/snap moment — it's inherently satisfying content
- Keep backgrounds contextual but not distracting
- Show real use cases (gym, desk, kitchen, gaming setup, bedroom)
- Include dimension/scale references somewhere in the set
- Make the metal finish VISIBLE — gloss, reflection, light catch = premium perception
- Use consistent color grading within each product's image set

### DON'T ❌
- Don't hide the licensed artwork behind a phone on the bottle
- Don't use cluttered backgrounds that compete with the product
- Don't over-edit/filter — Amazon shoppers want accurate representation
- Don't use low-res mockups — these need real photography on real products
- Don't put text on the Amazon hero image (violation = listing suppressed)
- Don't show competitor products in comparison shots (Amazon violation)
- Don't use lifestyle models without proper release forms
- Don't shoot wall art flat (straight-on top-down) — it looks like a poster, not premium metal

## ROOM MOCKUP SETTINGS REFERENCE

For lifestyle/room shots, here are the 4 target environments:

### 1. Gaming Setup (Wall Art primary)
- Dark walls (charcoal/black)
- RGB LED strips (purple/blue preferred)
- Dual monitors on desk
- Mechanical keyboard, gaming mouse
- Art centered above monitors
- **Mood:** Immersive, tech-forward, slightly moody

### 2. Modern Bedroom (Wall Art + Bottle)
- White or light grey walls
- Clean bed with neutral bedding (white/grey/beige)
- Nightstand with lamp, book, small plant
- Art above bed (centered) or beside bed
- Bottle on nightstand with phone attached
- **Mood:** Clean, aspirational, calm

### 3. Home Office / Desk (Bottle primary)
- Clean desk, laptop open
- Bottle with phone attached (video call angle)
- Small plant, notebook, pen cup
- Natural light from side (window feel)
- **Mood:** Productive, organized, modern professional

### 4. Gym (Bottle primary)
- Rubber gym floor
- Dumbbells, bench, or squat rack in soft focus background
- Bottle on floor, phone recording user
- Towel nearby, possibly water droplets on bottle
- **Mood:** Raw, authentic, energetic
