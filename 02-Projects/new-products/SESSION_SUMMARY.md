## Products

### 1. MagSafe Water Bottle
- **What:** Premium stainless steel insulated water bottle with integrated MagSafe phone mount/stand
- **Sizes:** 24oz and 32oz
- **Print methods:** UV DTF (full-color wraps) + Laser Engraving (premium etched designs)
- **Finished samples already produced:** Black/LFC laser, Red/LFC UV DTF, Pink/Peanuts UV DTF, Blue/WWE
- **Key feature:** The bottle IS the phone tripod — 15°-90° adjustable stand for recording workouts, video calls, recipes, content creation

### 2. Metal Wall Art
- **What:** 300×400mm (12×16") aluminium panels with UV-printed licensed artwork + magnetic wall mount
- **Material:** 1mm aluminium, gloss white coated, R2 rounded corners
- **Mount:** Magnetic system (steel plate on wall + magnet on panel) — no nails, no drilling, 10-second install
- **Comparable to:** Displate ($277M/year business)

## Key Strategic Insights

1. **Zero licensed competition** in both categories on Amazon — we're creating a new sub-category
2. **Cross-sell ecosystem** nobody can replicate — matching phone case + bottle + wall art in the same licensed design
3. **Blanks + printers in-house** = days to market, not months
4. **Displate's $277M proves the wall art market** — they just refuse to sell on Amazon
5. **MagSafe bottles are a TikTok-viral category** — gym recording angle is the #1 hook
6. **Laser engraved on matte black = genuinely luxury** — $45-50 price justified, best-looking product in category
7. **Wall art at $6.04 cost / $30 retail = 33-40% margins after Amazon** — better on DTC (60%)
