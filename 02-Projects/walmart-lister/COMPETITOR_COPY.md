# Competitor Copy Analysis — Phone Case Features

> **Purpose:** Identify copy elements we should use in our product descriptions
> **Date:** 2026-03-15

## Casetify — Premium Copy Points
- **Drop protection height:** Up to 21ft (Ultra Bounce), 8ft (Impact), 6.6ft (Bounce)
- **Military standard:** MIL-STD-810G — they say "3x/4x/6x/10x military standard"
- **Proprietary material:** "EcoShock™" — plant-based shock absorption material, absorbs 95% of impact
- **Newer material:** "TesLock" — more durable than EcoShock, increases longevity
- **Anti-yellowing:** "UV Defender technology — this case will never turn yellow"
- **MagSafe:** Compatible, strong magnetic lock
- **Layers:** "6 layers of 360° protection" (Ultra Bounce)
- **Sustainability:** Plant-based materials, eco-friendly angle
- **Testing language:** "26 consecutive drops from 4 feet" per MIL-STD grade
- **Price point:** $40-$100 (Ultra Bounce $100)

## ESR — Value Copy Points
- **Military grade:** "Shockproof Military-Grade Protection" (MIL-STD-810G claim)
- **Materials:** TPU + polycarbonate (same as our HTPCR/HB401)
- **MagSafe strength:** 12.2N pull / 15.9N push — 38% stronger than average
- **Corner protection:** Air pockets in all 4 corners, 3.1mm thickness
- **Dot matrix:** Anti-watermark dots prevent Newton rings between case and phone
- **Camera control:** Touch-sensitive camera button support (iPhone 17)
- **Camera protection:** Raised bezels around camera island
- **Screen protection:** Raised lip on all 4 corners
- **Lanyard cutout:** Included
- **Price point:** $8-15 (very competitive)

## OtterBox — Durability Copy Points
- **Drop protection:** Up to 7x military standard (Defender), 5x (Symmetry)
- **Multi-layer:** "Inner shell + outer slipcover" (Defender)
- **Antimicrobial:** "Silver-based antimicrobial additive" (Defender)
- **Port covers:** Dust/debris protection
- **Warranty:** Lifetime limited warranty

## What We Should Include (Head Case Designs)

### Technical Features for HTPCR/HB401:
1. **TPU bumper + PC hard back** — dual-material hybrid construction
2. **Integrated magnetic ring** — MagSafe compatible for wireless charging & accessories
3. **Military-grade protection** — if we have MIL-STD-810G testing (verify with Cem)
4. **Raised bezels** — screen and camera lens protection
5. **Air cushion corners** — enhanced drop protection (if applicable)
6. **Precise cutouts** — full access to all ports, buttons, and camera control
7. **Dot matrix interior** — prevents watermark effect (if applicable)
8. **Slim profile** — doesn't add bulk despite dual-material protection
9. **High-resolution UV printing** — scratch-resistant, won't fade or peel
10. **Officially Licensed** — authentic, authorized artwork

### What Competitors Say That We Don't:
1. **Drop height claims** — Casetify says "21ft", ESR says "military grade". We say nothing specific.
2. **MagSafe magnet strength** — ESR quantifies (12N pull). We don't mention magnets.
3. **Anti-yellowing** — Casetify has "UV Defender". We don't address yellowing.
4. **Camera control compatibility** — ESR highlights iPhone 17 camera button. We should too.
5. **Number of drops tested** — Casetify says "156 consecutive drops". Impressive claim.
6. **Material science** — Both competitors name their materials. We should too.
7. **Sustainability** — Casetify leads with plant-based materials. Opportunity if applicable.
8. **Warranty** — OtterBox has lifetime warranty. Do we offer any warranty?
