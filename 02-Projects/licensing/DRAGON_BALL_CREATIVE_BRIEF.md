# Creative Brief: Dragon Ball Z & Super

## 1. Market & Brand Context
- **Brand:** Dragon Ball Z / Dragon Ball Super (Toei Animation)
- **Status:** High growth trajectory ($34K/90d in recent PULSE analysis). Anime scaling fast.
- **Target Audience:** Shonen anime fans, nostalgia buyers (DBZ), current series fans (Super). 

## 2. Product Focus
- **Priority Form Factors:** Hybrid MagSafe (HB401), TPU+PC (HTPCR), Leather Wallets (HLBWH).
- **Expansion Forms:** MagSafe Water Bottles, Desk Mats (especially useful for wide landscape scenes).

## 3. Design Priorities
1. **Character Portraits:** Goku (Base, Super Saiyan, Ultra Instinct), Vegeta, Gohan, Frieza.
2. **Iconography:** Capsule Corp logo, Kame symbol, Dragon Balls.
3. **Action/Aura Scenes:** Super Saiyan transformations with vibrant energy auras (plays well on glass/glossy cases).

## 4. Execution Guidelines
- **Color Palette:** High contrast. Vibrant orange, glowing blues/yellows (auras), deep space blacks.
- **Placement:** Keep faces clear of the camera hole cluster. Center gravity for action poses.
- **Tone:** Action-packed, dynamic, iconic.

## 5. Required Deliverables
- [ ] 5x Hero Smart Objects (Goku UI, Vegeta SSGSS, Frieza, Capsule Corp, Group Shot).
- [ ] 1x Desk Mat template for a panoramic battle scene.
- [ ] Render 10 device variants (focus on IPH15/16/17 and S24 series) for each hero object to feed into ListingForge.
