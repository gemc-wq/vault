# Creative Brief: One Piece Phone Case Collection (HB401/HTPCR)
**Date:** 2026-03-29
**From:** Sven (Creative Director)
**To:** PH Creative Team (Jeffrey, Jessie, Danica, Ricardo)
**Property:** One Piece (Anime / Netflix Live Action)

## 1. Objective
Create high-quality, high-converting product listings for the One Piece launch on Amazon US. This is a "first mover" opportunity — no official competitors currently exist on Amazon US for this license.

## 2. Design Priority (Tier 1 - The "Hype" Designs)
Please prioritize these 5 designs for the first batch of smart object creation:

1.  **Gear 5 Luffy (Sun God Nika):** 
    - *Visuals:* White hair, joyful/laughing expression, cloud-like effects.
    - *Vibe:* Mythical, powerful, cartoonishly rubbery.
2.  **Luffy Wanted Poster:** 
    - *Visuals:* Classic 3 Billion Berry bounty poster texture.
3.  **Roronoa Zoro (Santoryu):** 
    - *Visuals:* Three-sword style action pose.
4.  **Netflix Live-Action Jolly Roger:** 
    - *Visuals:* Realistic Straw Hat skull logo from the Netflix series.
5.  **Trafalgar D. Water Law:** 
    - *Visuals:* "Room" effects or yellow aura aesthetic.

## 3. Product Types
- **HTPCR** (Hybrid Magsafe Case) — Top priority for Amazon US.
- **HB401** (Hard Case) — Secondary.

## 4. Required Assets (Per Design)
- **Lane 1 (Templates):** Design applied to IPH17PMAX, IPH17PRO, S25 Ultra templates.
- **Lane 2 (Hero Shots):** Front-facing product shot on pure white background (Walmart/Amazon spec).
- **Lane 4 (Marketing):** 1-2 lifestyle shots (e.g., street style or anime-fan desk setup).

## 5. Technical Specs
- Follow logo/legal placement rules: Ensure licensor legal lines (Toei Animation / Netflix) are readable but don't interfere with main art.
- No distortions on panel breaks for consoles (if applicable later).

## 6. Deadline
Draft mocks for Tier 1 designs by **Wednesday, April 1**.
